About

Friday’s Colour is the simple record of the first explorations of colour that we have made together. We have been exchanging photographs every Friday for the duration of one season from December to March, from solstice to equinox, from Advent 2023 to easter 2024. It is hard to say what or how we decide to send. Sometimes one of us will respond to the other on the day by going out in search of an image. At other times the pictures have a less deliberate connection. We approach from very different viewpoints - Helen is a scientist/mathematician-turned-painter and Elspeth is a potter whose three-dimensional work is only secondarily engaged with colour. So in some ways we are novices. We are intrigued to work together in a way that both consciously and unconsciously plays with colour - and then inevitably with light. In these images we are not making colour, as we are doing in our work with other materials, but finding it - or it is finding us. Perhaps we want to publish these pictures just to say: look around you and let your eyes forage for delights.
